WHAT IS MCC?

Founded in 2025 by creator Maemae, MCC is built to grow the global community of Korean speakers and spread love for Korean culture. We guide and support aspiring learners on their journey to fluency — together.

At MCC, we’re more than just learners — we’re a community helping each other reach Korean fluency, one step at a time.

Fluency Together, the Maemae way

MCC isn’t just a place to study — it’s a guided journey where every learner supports each other and we grow together.

Spark Connections

We aim to connect people through the beauty of the culture as well, encouraging meaningful friendships and great appreciation of Korea.

Your Korean Journey Starts Here

With step-by-step learning and community support, your journey to fluency begins the moment YOU join us.
